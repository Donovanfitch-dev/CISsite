About Us

Founder

Tab Core

Authorized Sales Representative

Tab Core brings over 25 years of hands-on experience in the industrial HVAC industry. Throughout his career, he has worked alongside owners, engineering firms, and contractors across Louisiana and the Gulf region, helping facilities select equipment that fits their actual operating conditions.

As an authorized representative of Shield Air Solutions and VTS Group, Tab specializes in Class 1 Division 2 (C1D2) rated equipment, general purpose equipment, and custom air handling units. His focus is practical, ensuring that the equipment selected meets compliance requirements, withstands the environment, and performs reliably over its full service life.

Tab founded Core Industrial Solutions because he saw too many projects where equipment was selected based on availability instead of fit. Facilities would end up with units that did not match the environment, leading to rework, safety concerns, and unnecessary cost. He believed there was a better way to serve the industry.

They deserve someone who will walk their facility, understand their constraints, and design a solution that actually fits, the first time.

That belief drives every project Core takes on. Whether it is a new installation, a replacement, or an upgrade, Tab brings the same approach: listen, assess, recommend the right equipment, and support the process from start to finish.

Services

Industrial HVAC Services Built on Experience

From facility walkthroughs to equipment selection and project coordination, we help you get the right Class 1 Division 2 (C1D2) and general purpose Industrial HVAC equipment the first time.

01

Site Walkthroughs and Requirements Gathering

The Problem

Many equipment problems start before a purchase order is written. Without a clear understanding of the facility environment, constraints, and installation needs, the wrong equipment gets specified.

Our Approach

We perform hands-on facility walkthroughs to assess the actual site conditions. This includes evaluating the operating environment, space constraints, access for installation and maintenance, compliance zones including Class 1 Division 2 (C1D2) areas, and any unique conditions that affect equipment performance.

The Outcome

You get a clear picture of what your facility actually needs before any equipment is selected, preventing costly mistakes and rework downstream.

02

Equipment Selection Support

The Problem

Selecting Industrial HVAC equipment based on catalogs or availability alone often leads to units that do not match the application. Undersized, oversized, or non-compliant equipment creates safety issues, wasted budget, and downtime.

Our Approach

We provide practical, experience-based recommendations that match equipment to your actual use case. This includes verifying Class 1 Division 2 (C1D2) requirements, sizing for real-world conditions, and aligning unit type and configuration with your facility and project goals.

The Outcome

You receive a recommendation you can trust, equipment that fits the environment, meets compliance needs, and is built for the long term.

03

Shield Air Solutions and VTS Group Equipment

The Problem

Industrial and petrochemical facilities often need specialized equipment that off-the-shelf options cannot provide, particularly in hazardous environments requiring C1D2 rated units or custom configurations.

Our Approach

As an authorized representative of Shield Air Solutions and VTS Group, we offer direct access to a full range of Industrial HVAC equipment. Shield Air Solutions manufactures Class 1 Division 2 (C1D2) rated equipment and general purpose units built for demanding industrial environments. VTS Group manufactures custom air handling units that can be configured to meet specific facility requirements.

The Outcome

You get access to purpose-built equipment from manufacturers who specialize in industrial applications, supported by a local representative who understands your facility.

04

Project Support and Coordination

The Problem

Equipment selection does not happen in isolation. Owners, engineering firms, and contractors all need to be aligned on what is being specified and why. Miscommunication leads to rework, delays, and equipment that does not fit.

Our Approach

We support the full selection process by working alongside all parties. From initial requirements through quoting and procurement, we help ensure that everyone is working from the same information and that the equipment selected fits the project scope.

The Outcome

Fewer surprises, less rework, and equipment that fits the environment and requirements from day one.

How It Works

A Straightforward Process

From initial facility walkthrough to final quote and support, we keep the process focused, practical, and transparent.

01

Walkthrough

Everything starts with a visit to your facility. We walk the site to understand the environment, assess existing conditions, identify constraints, and get a clear picture of what the project actually requires. This includes evaluating Class 1 Division 2 (C1D2) zones, access points, space limitations, and any conditions that will affect equipment selection.

What This Includes

  • On-site facility evaluation
  • Assessment of hazardous area classifications including C1D2
  • Documentation of physical and environmental constraints
  • Coordination with your team on access and scheduling
02

Requirements

Based on the walkthrough, we define the full set of equipment requirements. This means going beyond general specifications to capture the real-world factors that determine whether equipment will perform reliably in your environment.

What This Includes

  • Equipment sizing and capacity requirements
  • Compliance and classification needs, including Class 1 Division 2 (C1D2)
  • Environmental and operating condition specifications
  • Coordination with owners, engineering firms, and contractors
03

Recommend Equipment

We present practical, fit-for-purpose equipment options from Shield Air Solutions and VTS Group. Each recommendation is grounded in your actual facility conditions, not theoretical specs. We explain why a particular unit, configuration, or specification makes sense for your project.

What This Includes

  • Shield Air Solutions C1D2 and general purpose equipment options
  • VTS Group custom air handling unit configurations
  • Clear explanation of trade-offs and fit
  • Equipment matched to compliance, environment, and budget
04

Quote and Support

Once you have selected the right equipment, we deliver a clear quote and continue to support the process through procurement and coordination. Our involvement does not end with a recommendation. We stay engaged to help ensure the equipment is ordered correctly and arrives ready for installation.

What This Includes

  • Transparent quoting with no hidden extras
  • Procurement coordination and lead time management
  • Ongoing support through installation planning
  • Single point of contact throughout the process
